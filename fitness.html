TAKE YOUR BODY TO THE NEXT LEVEL

Browse through these physique changing exercises

Pull-Ups

Pull-Ups are a great exercise for building your back and biceps. grab a bar with your hands facing outward and pull yourself up until your chin is over the bar.

Box Jumps

If you want to build your leg muscles and increase your endurance, then box jumps are for you. Choose a box between 14-24 inches high and jump on top of the box, landing softly with your feet flat on the box.

Dumbbell Row

Rows are a great way to add size to your back. Lean at 60 a degree angle and pull the dumbbell up until it reaches your hip.

Deadlift

The deadlift is a staple lift in the bodybuilding community. It is an exercise that works the entire body, with emphasis on your back. Start with your feet shoulder width apart, with the bar on the ground and it in the middle of your feet. Slightly bend your knees, with your spine in a neutral position. Lift the bar using your legs and hips. Let the fun begin!

Weighted Sled Push

Weighted sled pushes are a great way to increase leg strength and build up endurance. Find a comfortable weight where you can push the sled for 20-30 seconds.

Sit-Ups

Core strength is integral to achieving your fitness goals. Your core stabilizes your body through all of its movements. Performing a a full sit-up works all of your abdominal muscles. Lay down on the floor with your knees bent and bring your chest up until you are an up right seated position.

Dumbbell Bicep Curl

The Dumbbell bicep curl is a classic exercise that can add size and definition to your arms. Start with your shoulders back and your arms by your side, fully extended. Lift the dumbbell up towards your shoulder without letting your arm sway forward.
